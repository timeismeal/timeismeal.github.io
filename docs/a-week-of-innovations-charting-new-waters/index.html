A Week of Innovations: Charting New Waters

Chad

16 Oct 2023 • 3 min read

Last week was nothing short of an exciting ride for Time is Meal. Armed with a vision and a thirst for innovation, I dived deep into the world of technology, exploring new avenues and tools to bring the best to the table. Here's a snapshot of the action-packed week:

Choosing the Right Back-end: Firebase vs. Supabase

The week started with me weighing the pros and cons of Firebase against Supabase. While Firebase offers a good range of services, my history with its authentication service has been a mixed bag. Moreover, with my strong familiarity with PostgreSQL, Supabase emerged as the clear winner. The platform's Row-Level Security policies and the ability to reflect real-time database changes on the web app were the cherries on top.

UI Makeover: Bootstrapping with Tailwind

After over a decade of loyalty to Bootstrap, it was time to flirt with something new: Tailwind. Partnering it with DaisyUI proved to be a match made in heaven. While Tailwind offered lightness and customizability, DaisyUI brought in the much-needed components. And guess who played matchmaker? My AI co-pilot, which not only suggested DaisyUI but also assisted me in mastering Tailwind best practices.

Database Shenanigans with Supabase

The AI continued to be my trusted ally, as I ventured into implementing the database in Supabase. From double-checking SQL codes to crafting functions and triggers, the AI was instrumental. For instance, when a new user is created in Supabase Authentication, a corresponding profile gets auto-created in my database, thanks to a nifty trigger the AI helped write SQL statements and proofread them.

Filling the Pantry

To ensure the web app had a rich database to work with, I enlisted the AI's expertise once again. It helped populate categories with a variety of foods and products, making sure users had a plethora to explore.

A Milestone Moment: Breathing Life into Time is Meal

The week's highlight was undoubtedly the successful integration of OpenAI's chat module. This was not just another feature addition; it was the realization of Time is Meal's core vision: to be AI-powered. The proof-of-concept marked a pivotal moment. It signifies that Time is Meal isn't just another app; it's the beginning of an AI-driven revolution in meal planning and grocery shopping. With this foundation laid, the sky's the limit. We're not stopping here; this is just the launchpad for the exciting innovations to come.

Giving Birth to Your AI Culinary Companion

Every great kitchen has a master chef, and for Time is Meal, we envisioned a digital chef — an AI entity that would assist users throughout their journey in the web app. This isn't just any ordinary chef; it's a blend of technology, creativity, and culinary expertise. The AI played a pivotal role in bringing this vision closer to reality in two significant ways:

  1. Naming the Chef: A name isn't just a label; it's an identity. With the AI's insights, we've chosen a name that encapsulates the essence of our digital chef. A name that resonates with culinary expertise and technological innovation.
  2. Crafting the Image: Beyond the name, we wanted our chef to have a face — an avatar that users could connect with. Using generative image AI, we've crafted an avatar that's both engaging and representative of our vision.

While we're bursting with excitement, we're holding onto the big reveal for now. Both the name and the avatar will be unveiled soon. So, keep those taste buds (and your curiosity) tingling!

Wrapping Up

In essence, last week was a testament to the power of collaboration, innovation, and the sheer potential that AI brings to the table. With foundational milestones achieved, Time is Meal is poised for exciting times ahead. Stay tuned!
