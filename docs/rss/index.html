Last week was nothing short of an exciting ride for Time is Meal. Armed with a vision and a thirst for innovation, I dived deep into the world of technology, exploring new avenues and tools to bring the best to the table. Here's a snapshot of the action-packed week:

Choosing the Right Back-end: Firebase vs. Supabase

The week started with me weighing the pros and cons of Firebase against Supabase. While Firebase offers a good range of services, my history with its authentication service has been a mixed bag. Moreover, with my strong familiarity with PostgreSQL, Supabase emerged as the clear winner. The platform's Row-Level Security policies and the ability to reflect real-time database changes on the web app were the cherries on top.

UI Makeover: Bootstrapping with Tailwind

After over a decade of loyalty to Bootstrap, it was time to flirt with something new: Tailwind. Partnering it with DaisyUI proved to be a match made in heaven. While Tailwind offered lightness and customizability, DaisyUI brought in the much-needed components. And guess who played matchmaker? My AI co-pilot, which not only suggested DaisyUI but also assisted me in mastering Tailwind best practices.

Database Shenanigans with Supabase

The AI continued to be my trusted ally, as I ventured into implementing the database in Supabase. From double-checking SQL codes to crafting functions and triggers, the AI was instrumental. For instance, when a new user is created in Supabase Authentication, a corresponding profile gets auto-created in my database, thanks to a nifty trigger the AI helped write SQL statements and proofread them.

Filling the Pantry

To ensure the web app had a rich database to work with, I enlisted the AI's expertise once again. It helped populate categories with a variety of foods and products, making sure users had a plethora to explore.

A Milestone Moment: Breathing Life into Time is Meal

The week's highlight was undoubtedly the successful integration of OpenAI's chat module. This was not just another feature addition; it was the realization of Time is Meal's core vision: to be AI-powered. The proof-of-concept marked a pivotal moment. It signifies that Time is Meal isn't just another app; it's the beginning of an AI-driven revolution in meal planning and grocery shopping. With this foundation laid, the sky's the limit. We're not stopping here; this is just the launchpad for the exciting innovations to come.

Giving Birth to Your AI Culinary Companion

Every great kitchen has a master chef, and for Time is Meal, we envisioned a digital chef — an AI entity that would assist users throughout their journey in the web app. This isn't just any ordinary chef; it's a blend of technology, creativity, and culinary expertise. The AI played a pivotal role in bringing this vision closer to reality in two significant ways:

  1. Naming the Chef: A name isn't just a label; it's an identity. With the AI's insights, we've chosen a name that encapsulates the essence of our digital chef. A name that resonates with culinary expertise and technological innovation.
  2. Crafting the Image: Beyond the name, we wanted our chef to have a face — an avatar that users could connect with. Using generative image AI, we've crafted an avatar that's both engaging and representative of our vision.

While we're bursting with excitement, we're holding onto the big reveal for now. Both the name and the avatar will be unveiled soon. So, keep those taste buds (and your curiosity) tingling!

Wrapping Up

In essence, last week was a testament to the power of collaboration, innovation, and the sheer potential that AI brings to the table. With foundational milestones achieved, Time is Meal is poised for exciting times ahead. Stay tuned!

When designing Time is Meal, I had to make a choice: go with the sleek, straight-to-the-point top navigation, or the robust, can-carry-everything side navigation. Ding, ding, ding! Let the match begin!

The Tale of the Tape

Top Navigation

  • Pros: Doesn't hog space, stays at the top where it's easy to spot.
  • Ideal for: Most websites, from tiny to huge. Especially those like mine that aim for simplicity and clarity.

Side Navigation

  • Pros: Perfect for when you have a lot to show. It's like the utility belt of web design.
  • Ideal for: More complex sites, where users might need a bit more guidance or have many options to choose from.

Given that Time is Meal's mission is straightforward – helping users whip up recipes and generate shopping lists – I leaned towards the top navigation. It's like choosing a quick stir-fry over a 5-course meal; both have their place, but one just felt right for now.

I know side navigation might be sulking in the corner, feeling a bit left out. But who knows? As Time is Meal grows and evolves, there might be a spot for it in the future. For now, though, top navigation is leading the dance.

To sum it up, it's all about picking the right tool for the job. And for Time is Meal's debut, top navigation was the chef's choice.

When the world was gripped by the pandemic, it changed how we lived in many ways. For me, it amplified a pain point that had been simmering for a while: grocery shopping.

Checkout in a Name? Unpacking the Brand

There's a popular saying, "time is money," highlighting how precious our time is. In the same vein, I realized that every minute I spent stressing over groceries and meal planning was a minute I could have spent on something valuable. Thus, the idea of "Time is Meal" was born. Time, like a well-cooked meal, is precious and should be savored, not wasted on tedious tasks.

The Pandemic's Grocery Challenge

Before the pandemic, forgetting an ingredient from the grocery list was a painful inconvenience. But during the pandemic, it became a significant stressor. Every trip to the store mattered. Every item on the list was crucial. Yet, week after week, I found myself overlooking essential ingredients. Sometimes, the forgotten items were just minor additions. Other times, they were the heart of the meal. Going back to the store, especially in those times, was the last thing I wanted.

Family Favorites and Indecisiveness

Meal planning had always been a hot topic in my household. With diverse tastes and preferences, reaching a consensus was an Olympic sport. Sure, there are numerous meal planning apps out there. But most of them offered new recipes or comprehensive meal plans. That wasn't what I needed. I had a repertoire of family favorites, dishes my kids loved, and tried-and-true recipes that had been passed down through generations. I wasn't looking for new recipes; I wanted a way to quickly select our beloved meals, generate a grocery list, and ensure I never forgot an ingredient in one single trip.

The Birth of Time is Meal

With this need in mind, I set out one weekend to craft a solution. The result? The first version of Time is Meal. It was basic, a little rough around the edges, but it did exactly what I needed. For three years, I used this home-baked app. It saved me countless hours, reduced the number of trips to the grocery store, and, best of all, diminished the family quarrels over meal choices.

AI-Powered Evolution

Though the original Time is Meal has served me well, the world of tech never stands still. And neither do I. As I embark on crafting version 2.0, I'm integrating the power of artificial intelligence to make it even smarter, more intuitive, and tailored to serve a wider audience. "One list. One trip. Every ingredient." remains the core mantra, but with AI's touch, it's shaping up to be more insightful and user-centric than ever.

Journeying Together

But here's the best part: I'm not on this journey alone. I'm bringing all of you along with me. Through this blog, you'll be by my side as I navigate the challenges, celebrate the milestones, and refine this tool. We'll explore the fascinating world of AI, uncover its potential in enhancing everyday tasks, and together, we'll witness the evolution of Time is Meal from a personal solution to a global asset.

In Conclusion

From a simple weekend project to an AI-enhanced revolution, Time is Meal stands as a testament to what personal determination and technology can achieve. As we move forward, I'm thrilled to share every step of this evolution with you. Let's make meal planning and grocery shopping not just efficient, but also enjoyable!
