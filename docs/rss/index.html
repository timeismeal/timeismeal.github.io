When the world was gripped by the pandemic, it changed how we lived in many ways. For me, it amplified a pain point that had been simmering for a while: grocery shopping.

Checkout in a Name? Unpacking the Brand

There's a popular saying, "time is money," highlighting how precious our time is. In the same vein, I realized that every minute I spent stressing over groceries and meal planning was a minute I could have spent on something valuable. Thus, the idea of "Time is Meal" was born. Time, like a well-cooked meal, is precious and should be savored, not wasted on tedious tasks.

The Pandemic's Grocery Challenge

Before the pandemic, forgetting an ingredient from the grocery list was a painful inconvenience. But during the pandemic, it became a significant stressor. Every trip to the store mattered. Every item on the list was crucial. Yet, week after week, I found myself overlooking essential ingredients. Sometimes, the forgotten items were just minor additions. Other times, they were the heart of the meal. Going back to the store, especially in those times, was the last thing I wanted.

Family Favorites and Indecisiveness

Meal planning had always been a hot topic in my household. With diverse tastes and preferences, reaching a consensus was an Olympic sport. Sure, there are numerous meal planning apps out there. But most of them offered new recipes or comprehensive meal plans. That wasn't what I needed. I had a repertoire of family favorites, dishes my kids loved, and tried-and-true recipes that had been passed down through generations. I wasn't looking for new recipes; I wanted a way to quickly select our beloved meals, generate a grocery list, and ensure I never forgot an ingredient in one single trip.

The Birth of Time is Meal

With this need in mind, I set out one weekend to craft a solution. The result? The first version of Time is Meal. It was basic, a little rough around the edges, but it did exactly what I needed. For three years, I used this home-baked app. It saved me countless hours, reduced the number of trips to the grocery store, and, best of all, diminished the family quarrels over meal choices.

AI-Powered Evolution

Though the original Time is Meal has served me well, the world of tech never stands still. And neither do I. As I embark on crafting version 2.0, I'm integrating the power of artificial intelligence to make it even smarter, more intuitive, and tailored to serve a wider audience. "One list. One trip. Every ingredient." remains the core mantra, but with AI's touch, it's shaping up to be more insightful and user-centric than ever.

Journeying Together

But here's the best part: I'm not on this journey alone. I'm bringing all of you along with me. Through this blog, you'll be by my side as I navigate the challenges, celebrate the milestones, and refine this tool. We'll explore the fascinating world of AI, uncover its potential in enhancing everyday tasks, and together, we'll witness the evolution of Time is Meal from a personal solution to a global asset.

In Conclusion

From a simple weekend project to an AI-enhanced revolution, Time is Meal stands as a testament to what personal determination and technology can achieve. As we move forward, I'm thrilled to share every step of this evolution with you. Let's make meal planning and grocery shopping not just efficient, but also enjoyable!

Today, I'm introducing you to a project that's been brewing in my mind for quite some time: Time is Meal.

Like many of you, I've faced the weekly conundrum of meal planning. The long hours spent deciding on meals, the tedious task of jotting down every ingredient, and that sinking feeling when you realize you forgot the most crucial item (like butter, perhaps?) halfway through cooking. This repetitive cycle not only disrupts our culinary adventures but often strains our relationships with loved ones over something as simple as a grocery list.

Enter Time is Meal - an app designed to transform your grocery shopping experience. But this isn't just another meal planning app. This is a blend of personal pain points, cutting-edge AI technology, and a vision to make every mealtime a joyous occasion.

Why AI?

You might wonder, "Why bring AI into meal planning?" Well, the potential of AI to understand nuanced personal preferences, dietary restrictions, and to predict and plan based on patterns is immense. I envision a tool where AI doesn't just suggest meals but understands you. Where it doesn't just create a grocery list but ensures it's exhaustive, down to the last sprinkle of salt.

AI as My Co-Pilot

As I delve into the development of Time is Meal, AI will be my trusty sidekick. From assisting in writing and infusing creativity into the app's design to helping troubleshoot coding challenges, AI will play a pivotal role. I aim to document this dual journey: building an app with AI and building an AI-powered app.

So, as I embark on this exciting expedition, I invite you to join me. Together, we'll explore, innovate, and hopefully, by the end, enjoy more fulfilling meals and less stressful grocery trips.
